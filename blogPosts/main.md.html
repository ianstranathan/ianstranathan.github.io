**Noodling around in Math, Physics & Computer Graphics** Ian Stranathan This site is a small, organziational resource and portfolio. It's mostly a personal linking archive for the various things I become interested in and work on. About ============================================== Me --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- I'm motivated by understanding how things work and I believe there is a deep difference between knowing something and understanding it. What you cannot create, you do not understand, to wit: from scratch. Starting from scratch is almost always confusing. Sometimes, while trying to come to terms with that confusion, I write about it and an article finds its way here. Problem solving and learning are not for the special few, it only takes an open mind and a sense of curiosity. Site --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- I use the fantastic and free [Markdeep](http://casual-effects.com/markdeep/) library for formatting. The landing page is there to remind me to play around with shaders more often (grease the groove). They're just random shaders for fun, quality will vary.
