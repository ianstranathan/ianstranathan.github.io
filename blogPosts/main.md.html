About ======================================================================================= I'm motivated by understanding how things work and communicating ideas. I believe there is a deep difference between knowing something and understanding it. What you cannot create, you do not understand, to wit: from scratch. Starting from scratch is almost always confusing. Sometimes, it helps to write an article about it.
