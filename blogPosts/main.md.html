**Noodling around in Math, Physics & Computer Graphics** Ian Stranathan This is my home page. It's mostly a personal archive for the various things I become interested in and work on. About ======================================================================================= I'm motivated by understanding how things work and communicating ideas. I believe there is a deep difference between knowing something and understanding it. What you cannot create, you do not understand, to wit: from scratch. Starting from scratch is almost always confusing. Sometimes, while trying to come to terms with that confusion, some writing finds its way here. Site --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- I use the [Markdeep](http://casual-effects.com/markdeep/) library for minimalistic formatting. The landing page is just random shaders for fun, quality will vary.
