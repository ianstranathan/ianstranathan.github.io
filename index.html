Greetings, I'm Ian and this a small archive for things I become interested in (a poor man's Hyperphysics or Algorithm Archive)

I studied Physics (B.S.) and Math (B.S) and Mechanical Engineering (almost B.S) and I'm a hobbyist graphics programmer & game developer.

I'm motivated by understanding how things work and communicating ideas.

Writing doesn't just communicate ideas; it generates them. – Paul Graham

I use writing as scaffolding when trying to learn or understand something. Bottom up writing for the odd derivation or technical subject I'm confused about.

Sometimes that process turns into a full post here.

Unfortunately, I don't always have the time to proof read as thoroughly as I'd like - there are lots of spelling and grammar mistakes, but hopefully not too many technical ones.

Thanks for stopping by and feel free to contact me. I'm happy to hear from you. Cheers!
